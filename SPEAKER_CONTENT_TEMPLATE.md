## Questions?

If you have questions about what content to provide:

1. **Short bio:** Think of it as a Twitter bio - concise and punchy
2. **Medium bio:** What you'd put on a conference speaker page
3. **Full bio:** Your complete professional background and achievements
4. **Testimonials:** Ask past event organizers or attendees for quotes
5. **Videos:** Link to any recordings of your talks on YouTube or Vimeo
6. **Photos:** Professional headshots or candid photos from speaking events
